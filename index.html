About Our Lodge

Village 315 Lodge is dedicated to the craft of Freemasonry and the principles of self-improvement, charitable work, and brotherhood. We welcome men of good character who seek to enhance their lives through masonic education and fellowship.

1

Brotherly Love

We strengthen our bonds through mutual respect, support, and genuine care for our fellow members and the community we serve.

2

Relief

We are committed to providing assistance to those in need, both within our lodge family and throughout our broader community.

3

Truth

We pursue knowledge, wisdom, and integrity in all our endeavors, seeking enlightenment through education and honest reflection.

The Three Degrees

1°

Entered Apprentice

The foundation of masonic knowledge. The candidate is introduced to the principles of the craft and begins their journey toward enlightenment.

2°

Fellowcraft

The candidate deepens their understanding of masonic science and geometry. Knowledge of the craft is further developed and refined.

3°

Master Mason

The highest degree in the lodge. The Master Mason represents the completion of one's journey and the attainment of masonic wisdom.
